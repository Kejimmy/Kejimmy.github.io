Chuhan Wu

Chuhan Wu is now a Ph.D. candidate with the Department of Electronic Engineering at Tsinghua University, Beijing, China. His current research interests include recommender systems, user modeling and social media mining. He has published several papers on conferences and journals in AI, NLP and data mining fields.
