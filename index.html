Chuhan Wu

Chuhan Wu is now a Ph.D student from the Department of Electronic Engineering in Tsinghua University, Beijing, China. His current research interests include recommender systems, user modeling and social media mining. He has published several papers on conferences and journals in AI, NLP and data mining fields.

Chuhan Wu received the B.E. degree from the Electronic Engineering Department of Tsinghua University.
